Bearing

The field almanac — your region's nature, offline.

Bearing is an offline regional nature dashboard: sun & moon, tides, weather, meteor showers, phenology, hardiness zones, migratory birds, pollen, UV, and foraging & fishing seasons — from public-domain U.S. government data.

  • A dozen public-data corpora, bundled for offline reference
  • Today's almanac for your region at a glance
  • Widgets and an Apple Watch companion

No account. No subscription. No ads. Nothing is collected — your settings stay on your device. Data is from NOAA, the National Weather Service, USFWS, USA-NPN, the USDA, the EPA, and the CDC (public domain). Bearing is not affiliated with or endorsed by any of these agencies.
