Swaydar

One scenario a day. Spot the sway.

Swaydar is a daily, on-device persuasion-deduction puzzle. Every UTC day, every player gets the same real-world pitch — a sales close, a dark pattern, a recruiting script, a breakup speech. You get four guesses to identify the stack of persuasion levers and name the keystone: the one lever whose removal would have collapsed the pitch at the moment of decision.

App Store Google Play

How it works

  1. Read today's scenario. Same one for every player worldwide.
  2. Stake up to four guesses on the persuasion levers in play.
  3. Name the keystone — the one lever the pitch can't survive without — and share a spoiler-free result grid.

What's inside

  • One scenario a day, free forever. Synchronized worldwide on UTC.
  • $1.99 one-time Pro unlock. Full daily history plus the per-scenario counterfactual lattice. No subscription.
  • 39 launch scenarios spanning sales, dark patterns, recruiting, negotiation, and personal-life pitches.
  • 39 locales out of the box. Engine and corpus shipped 1:1 with the Android port.
  • Home-screen + lock-screen widget. Streak and today's status without opening the app.
  • No ads, no analytics, no accounts, no network. Scenarios are bundled in the binary; nothing phones home.
