Cari Art Design Face Data Privacy

Effective July 1, 2026.

In One Line

Your photos and the faces in them are processed on your device and stay on your device.

Face Data And Photos

When you take or choose a photo, Cari Art Design uses the image only to make your caricature on your device. The app may use on-device face detection to find a face rectangle and facial landmark points such as eyes, nose, mouth, and face outline.

Your source photo, the detected face rectangle, and facial landmark points are handled in app memory during the caricature process. They are not uploaded, sold, shared, or sent to the developer or any third party. The app has no developer server, account system, analytics service, ad network, or tracking SDK that could receive this information.

Face detection is used only to confirm a usable face is present, center and crop the photo, guide camera framing, reshape the image into a caricature, and render the selected art style locally. It is not used for identity, authentication, face matching, ads, analytics, tracking, or profiling.

Storage And Retention

The app does not retain the source photo or facial landmark points after the drawing flow is complete. If you keep a finished souvenir, the finished artwork and optional time-lapse are stored only in the app's private storage on your device. You can delete souvenirs in the app, remove exported copies from Photos or shared destinations, or delete the app to remove app data.

Network Use

Network access is limited to Apple-hosted App Store services such as in-app purchase validation and Background Asset model-pack delivery. Those downloads do not include user photos, face data, or personal content.
