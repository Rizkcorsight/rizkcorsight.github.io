Foldcast

Foldcast is a quiet daily paper-folding puzzle. Fold the paper, punch the stack, then pick the pattern it makes when opened.

Private by design

Foldcast has no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no tracking. Progress, streaks, daily state, practice state, and trial timing stay on your device.
