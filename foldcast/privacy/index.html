Purchases

Foldcast is free to download with 3 days of full access. Starting that 3-day access window happens locally on your device and does not create a subscription or charge. After the 3 days, a single optional one-time purchase unlocks the whole app permanently — there is no subscription and nothing auto-renews or auto-charges. That purchase or restore action is handled entirely by Apple’s App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) through their own in-app purchase systems. Foldcast never sees or receives your name, payment card, or billing details. The developer receives only anonymous, aggregate sales figures from Apple and Google — never anything tied to you. Apple’s and Google’s handling of the transaction is governed by their own privacy policies.

Foldcast - Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Foldcast does not collect personal data. The app has no account system, no ads, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking. Rizk Corsight, LLC does not receive your puzzle progress, streaks, practice state, device identifiers, or usage history.

Data stored on your device

Foldcast stores daily progress, streaks, practice state, unlock state, and trial timing locally on your device. Deleting the app removes local app data according to your operating system's normal behavior.

Network and accounts

Foldcast does not require an account and does not use a developer backend for gameplay. The puzzle experience is designed to work offline.

Purchases

Foldcast offers a local full-access trial and a one-time purchase to unlock the app permanently. Apple App Store or Google Play processes payments, receipts, refunds, and billing information under its own privacy policy. Foldcast receives only the entitlement status needed to unlock the app; we do not receive your payment card, billing address, or transaction history.

Sharing

If you choose to share puzzle results, the app hands that content to your device's system share sheet. You choose the destination, and that destination handles the shared copy under its own privacy practices.

Children

Foldcast does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.
