Cari Art Design Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026
Developer: Rizk Corsight, LLC
Contact: rizkcorsight@rizkcorsight.com

Cari Art Design turns a photo into a caricature souvenir on your own device. Your photos and the faces in them are processed on your device and stay on your device. The app collects no personal data, shows no ads, has no accounts, and includes no third-party tracking.

Privacy Summary

The app does not ask for, gather, or store your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, identifiers, or any profile about you. There is no login and no developer server. Your source photo and finished caricature are not uploaded for processing.

The only network activity is downloading optional art model packs through the platform's own asset delivery service. These downloads carry no photo, face, or personal content from you.

Purchases are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play. The app does not receive your billing details; the developer receives only aggregate store reporting.

Permissions

Camera is used only if you choose to take a new photo in the app. Network access is used only for model-pack downloads. Notifications may be used to show progress for a long, user-requested model download or generation session.
