PartyPilot

AI Party Concierge

PartyPilot turns a rough hosting note into a practical local party plan. Start with a guest estimate, add optional manual RSVP rows, adjust quantities, review grouped shopping lists, estimate budget, draft reminders, and prepare invite copy.

What It Does

  • Builds party plans from rough host notes
  • Tracks adult and kid guest counts
  • Supports optional local RSVP rows with allergy notes
  • Recalculates food, drink, cleanup, and budget estimates
  • Exports shopping Markdown, CSV, and host briefs
  • Lets hosts share invite copy only when they choose

Privacy

No account, ads, analytics, or tracking. PartyPilot does not collect Contacts and does not send messages, create carts, contact vendors, or perform outreach automatically.
