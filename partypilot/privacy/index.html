PartyPilot Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Overview

PartyPilot is a local-first party planning app for iPhone and iPad. It helps hosts draft plans, shopping lists, budgets, reminders, invite copy, and optional manual RSVP rows.

Data Collected

Rizk Corsight does not collect personal data from PartyPilot. The app has no account system, no analytics SDK, no ads, and no tracking SDK.

Data Stored On Device

PartyPilot stores party intake text, generated plans, manual RSVP rows, local quantity edits, unlock status, and local trial state on the user's device. The trial start date may be mirrored in Keychain so normal app data deletion does not reset the local trial clock.

Contacts And Sharing

PartyPilot does not access the user's Contacts database. Invite text, shopping lists, CSV files, and host briefs are generated for manual export or manual sharing. The app does not send messages, create carts, contact vendors, perform outreach, or schedule reminders automatically.

Purchases

PartyPilot uses one non-consumable StoreKit product for a lifetime unlock. Purchases and restore actions are handled by Apple. Rizk Corsight does not receive payment card details.

Network Use

PartyPilot has no app-specific backend. StoreKit may contact Apple services for product metadata, purchase, restore, and entitlement verification.
